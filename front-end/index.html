Partnership

Welcome to Sabri Trader. As a premier producer and exporter of B2B sportswear, teamwear, and custom athletic apparel, we bring modern manufacturing precision to the world of sports. Located in the manufacturing heart of Sialkot, Pakistan, we bridge the gap between raw industrial capacity and premium retail finishing.

One of our key strengths is catering to the specific needs of our customers by offering low Minimum Order Quantities (MOQ) for custom designs. This allows sports teams, clubs, gyms, and emerging brands to have their own unique branding on jerseys, shorts, gloves, and other sports accessories without having to place massive wholesale orders.

Our manufacturing process is characterized by precision, attention to detail, and the use of advanced technologies. We are not just a trading house; we are the factory floor. We maintain a highly skilled workforce that ensures every product is meticulously crafted to meet the highest standards of quality and durability.

Sabri Trader’s commitment to customer satisfaction goes beyond just manufacturing. We strive to establish long-lasting relationships with our clients globally by providing excellent customer service, timely delivery, and highly competitive pricing. Whether your focus is soccer, basketball, cricket, or any other discipline, we are well-equipped to meet your unique requirements and exceed your expectations.
