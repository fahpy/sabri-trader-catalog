About Us

Sabri Trader is a premier producer and exporter operating from the manufacturing heart of Sialkot, Pakistan. We don’t just make garments — we build brands. We are not just a trading house; we are the factory floor.

Why Partner With Sabri Trader?

Low Minimums (MOQ)

We cater to the specific needs of our customers by offering flexible, low Minimum Order Quantities for custom designs. This allows sports teams, clubs, gyms, and emerging brands to have their own unique branding without massive wholesale commitments.

End-to-End Service

From selecting high-quality raw materials to precision stitching, private label finishing, and final delivery, our systematic approach ensures excellence. We bridge the gap between raw industrial capacity and premium retail finishing.

Quality Assurance

Our manufacturing process is characterized by precision and attention to detail. Every product is meticulously crafted to meet the highest standards of quality and durability. Because "Quality is our Policy," we ensure export-grade stitching and heavy-duty fabric architecture.

Ethical Standards

We prepare our products keeping in view international standards and the requirements of professionals. We are fully committed to responsible and ethical manufacturing practices across our entire supply chain.

A Fully Certified & Export-Ready Manufacturer

We are well-equipped to meet your unique requirements and exceed your expectations, offering excellent customer service, highly competitive pricing, and timely delivery. We operate as a dedicated manufacturing partner for activewear startups, fitness studios, and professional teams across all disciplines including soccer, basketball, and cricket. Whether your focus is on a small custom batch or high-volume wholesale distribution, you receive the same dedicated focus.
